# shipmaths
Based on syllabus of British Officer of the Watch (OOW) certification (MCA). Ship calculations (sailings,routing,celestial navigation and stability). Get answers, without workings. Perfect for the busy watchkeeper or student who wishes to verify their answers (not viable to cheat without workings)
